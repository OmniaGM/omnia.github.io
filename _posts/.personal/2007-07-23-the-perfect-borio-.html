Well I have been ages searching for the perfect borio

for people who don’t know .. Borio is a drink mainly made of the well known biscuits “Borio”

After testing Casablanca’s and Karbozy’s … I figured out my own Borio

Get 2 Big Borios , and 1 small ( 15 pieces of Borio )

more than a half Glass of Milk

1 Chixo , and some Nesquik

Now prepare 13 of the Borios, Chixo ( not all of it .. leave some), some Nesquik , may be sugar too
Put them all in your Mixer .. and Mix :D

here is the trick .. how much should you leave the Mixer .. I think it depends .. but don’t leave it too much

Now .. Get your self a nice Glass ;)

You may put at the top of the Borio some Chixo , and the remaining 2 Borios after breaking then into small pieces

Enjoy your Borio ;)
